# Unexpected Sound Full Kit

A compact experimental sample kit built around contrast:
dark, bright, broken, cinematic, alien, liquid, and ugly-beautiful textures.

## Included
- 7 stereo loops
- 10 one-shots
- 4 stereo demo stems
- manifest.json

## Intended use
This is a creative starter kit for chopping, layering, pitching, and resampling.
The loops are intentionally different from each other so you can collide styles fast.

## Folder map
- loops/
- one_shots/drums/
- one_shots/tones/
- stems/demo_loop/

## Suggested workflow
1. Drag a loop into your DAW.
2. Layer one deep kick and one hat from the drum shots.
3. Add a zap or glass one-shot for accent transitions.
4. Chop the demo stems if you want pieces instead of full textures.

## Format
- WAV
- 44.1 kHz
- Loops are stereo
- Drum one-shots are mono
